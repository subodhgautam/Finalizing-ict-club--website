About Us

Who We Are

The ICT Club is a vibrant community of learners, innovators, and creators who share a deep passion for technology. We aim to equip students with modern digital skills — from programming and cybersecurity to robotics, AI, and data science.

Our Mission

We believe technology is best learned through doing. The club bridges the gap between classroom theory and real-world innovation. Our mission is to help every student discover, build, and grow through hands-on projects, collaboration, and mentorship.

What We Do

  • Conduct workshops, hackathons, and seminars on emerging tech.
  • Build digital tools and platforms to solve real problems.
  • Represent our school in national-level competitions.
  • Encourage teamwork, leadership, and creative thinking.

Together, we’re not just learning technology — we’re creating it.
